These details are captured from the shipment header in Oracle Transportation Management and added to the HDOWNER table F_SHIPMENT_DETAIL.

The following visualizations are available in this project:

Emission Summary

This visualization displays a summary of Actual CO2 Emission, Planned CO2 Emission, Actual CO2e Emission, and Planned CO2e Emission for the past six month. Also, it captures transportation mode wise CO2 and CO2e emission.

Planned and Actual Emission

This is a tabular visualization which captures the metrics Weight, Weight Distance, CO2 and CO2e emission, and Freight Intensity.

Freight Emission Intensity Month on Month

This visualization captures month wise planned and actual freight intensity for the last six months.
