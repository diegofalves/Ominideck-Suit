Tracking Event Manager: Equipment Details

This page is accessed via:

  • Fleet Management > Visibility > Tracking Event. Click the Equipment tab and then click New Equipment.
  • Shipment Management > Visibility > Tracking Event. Click the Equipment tab and then click New Equipment.
  • Shipment Tracking Events. Click New Equipment.

Equipment ID

  1. Select a Shipment Equipment ID.
  2. Enter an Equipment Initial.
  3. Enter an Equipment Number.
  4. Enter an Equipment Initial Number.
  5. Enter the AAR Car Type. The AAR Car Type is also known as Equipment Type Code.
  6. Enter an Equipment ID.
  7. Select an Equipment Reference Number Qualifier ID.
  8. Enter an Equipment Refnum Value.
  9. If the equipment is a unit train, use the Equipment Index field to indicate which car will be used. For example, if the shipment will be in car 14 of the unit train, enter 14 in the Equipment Index field.

    Note: Shipment equipment is sorted by equipment index and sub-sorted by Pickup Stop Number.

  10. Enter a Chassis Initial.
  11. Enter a Chassis Number.
  12. Enter a Chassis Initial Number.

Equipment Information

  1. Enter an Equipment Type ID.
  2. Enter an Intermodal Equipment Length.
  3. Enter the Tare Weight of the equipment.
  4. Enter a Scale Name.
  5. Enter the Scale Location.
  6. Enter the Scale Ticket.
  7. Enter the Scale Weight.
  8. Select a Weight Qualifier for the scale weight.

Equipment Seals

  1. Enter a Seal Sequence.
  2. Enter a Seal Number.
  3. Click Save for each seal sequence you define.
  4. Click Save.
