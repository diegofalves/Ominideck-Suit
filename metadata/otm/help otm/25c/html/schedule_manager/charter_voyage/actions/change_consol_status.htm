Operational Planning

Change Consol Status

This page is accessed via:

  • Operational Planning > Schedule Management > Charter Voyage > Actions > Operational Planning > Change Buy Status and Change Sell Status
  • Shipment Management > Group Management > Consols > Actions > Shipment Management > Change Consol Status

Use these actions to change various statuses for the consol and consol shipment that is linked to a charter voyage.

Choose one of the following status actions to update consol status:

Status Action

Description

Resulting Consol/Shipment Status Value

Close

All consol statuses must be OPEN.

The consol shipment Total Weight and Volume are copied to the consol Booked Weight & Volume fields, if they were empty.

If UNITIZED, the shipment TEU and Â FEU counts are copied to the consol Booked TEU and Â FEU fields, if they were blank.

Orders can not be added to the consol when it is closed.

BOOKING_CLOSED

The shipment that is linked to the consol will also have its status set to SECURE RESOURCES_ACCEPTED and REVIEWED_EXECUTE.

Commit

All consol statuses must be READY-TO-COMMIT.

COMMITTING_COMMITTED

Open

All consol statuses must be CLOSED.

Orders can now be added to the consol.

BOOKING_OPEN

Consol shipment status is RE-PLAN & BOOKED

Precommit

All consol statuses must be NOT COMMITTED.

Consol shipment total weight & volume are copied to the consol Committed Weight and Volume fields, if they were blank.

If UNITIZED, consol shipment TEU & FEU counts are copied to consol Committed TEU and Â FEU fields, if they were blank.

You can now edit Committed field values on consol (you cannot under any other status).

COMMITTING_READY-TO-COMMIT

Uncommit

All consol statuses must be COMMITTED.

COMMITTING_READY-TO-COMMIT

Unprecommit

All consol statuses must be READY-TO-COMMIT.

COMMITTING_NOT COMMITTED
