Email Size Limitations

There are limits on the size of emails that can be sent from the Transportation and Global Trade Management service. For emails with attachments the limit is 10MB. Emails larger than these limits will still be sent, but the content/attachments will be converted to a link that the recipient can click on to retrieve the content.

For emails sent directly from BIPublisher, the email size is limited to 60MB.
