Multistop diagnostics provide insights into the performance of multi-stop savings algorithms, especially useful for identifying and analyzing potential sub-optimal solutions.Â This functionality helps you to understand the complex decision-making process behind multi-stop logistics and optimize their strategies when savings algorithm is used.
